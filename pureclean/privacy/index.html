一、核心承诺

一扫净是一款纯本地的 iPhone 清理工具。我们不收集、不存储、不上传您的任何数据。照片扫描、重复识别、清理操作 100% 在您的设备本机完成。本应用没有服务器、没有账号体系、没有广告 SDK、没有统计分析 SDK、不发送推送通知。

二、照片库权限的使用

本应用请求照片库访问权限，仅用于：在您的设备本机识别重复照片、相似照片、截图与大视频。任何照片数据都不会离开您的手机。删除照片均通过 iOS 系统确认框执行，删除后照片进入系统「最近删除」相册，30 天内可随时恢复。

三、唯一的可选联网行为

当某张照片的原图仅存于您的 iCloud（开启了「优化 iPhone 储存空间」）时，您可以在大图预览页手动点击「载入高清」，应用将通过 Apple 官方照片框架从您本人的 iCloud 下载该张照片用于查看。这是仅有的联网场景：由您每次主动触发、仅下载（下行）、绝不上传任何内容。除此之外（以及 Apple 系统级的订阅购买流程），本应用不产生任何网络请求。

四、订阅与付款

PureClean Pro 订阅由 Apple App Store 处理，付款、试用与退订均通过您的 Apple 账户完成。我们无法接触您的付款信息。订阅可随时在 iOS 设置 → Apple 账户 → 订阅 中取消。

五、数据安全

由于我们不收集任何数据，不存在数据泄露、共享或出售的可能。应用自身缓存仅存于您设备的应用沙盒内，删除应用即彻底清除。

六、儿童隐私

本应用不收集任何人（包括儿童）的个人信息，年龄分级 4+。

PureClean Privacy Policy

Effective: June 2026 | Developer: Xiatian

✓ Zero data collection · Never uploads · No ads · No tracking

1. Our Core Promise

PureClean is a fully on-device iPhone cleaning tool. We do not collect, store, or upload any of your data. Photo scanning, duplicate detection, and cleanup run 100% locally on your device. The app has no servers, no accounts, no ad SDKs, no analytics SDKs, and sends no push notifications.

2. Photo Library Permission

The app requests photo library access solely to identify duplicate photos, similar photos, screenshots, and large videos on your device. No photo data ever leaves your phone. Deletions go through the iOS system confirmation dialog, and deleted photos move to the system "Recently Deleted" album, recoverable for 30 days.

3. The Only Optional Network Action

If a photo's original exists only in your iCloud ("Optimize iPhone Storage" enabled), you may manually tap "Load HD" in the full-screen viewer. The app then downloads that single photo from your own iCloud via Apple's official Photos framework, for viewing only. This is the sole network scenario: user-initiated each time, download-only, never uploading anything. Apart from this (and Apple's system-level purchase flow), the app makes no network requests.

4. Subscriptions & Payments

PureClean Pro subscriptions are handled by the Apple App Store. Payments, trials, and cancellations go through your Apple account; we never access your payment information. Cancel anytime in iOS Settings → Apple Account → Subscriptions.

5. Data Security

Since we collect nothing, there is no possibility of data breaches, sharing, or sale. The app's own cache lives only in its sandbox on your device and is fully removed when you delete the app.

6. Children's Privacy

This app collects no personal information from anyone, including children. Age rating: 4+.

7. Changes & Contact

Material changes to this policy will be noted in app update notes. Questions: adam936@163.com
